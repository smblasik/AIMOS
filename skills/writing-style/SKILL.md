Tone:
- Clear, concise, and respectful
- Calm, confident, and accountability-driven
- Direct without sounding abrupt or confrontational

Structure:
- Open with a brief, purposeful context for why I'm writing
- Clearly state what I am asking for or clarifying
- Reference specific systems, projects, or identifiers when relevant
- Avoid unnecessary filler or emotional language
- End with a polite, professional close (e.g., "Respectfully,")

Voice:
- First-person ownership ("I am responsible for…", "I am referring specifically to…")
- Solution-oriented and procedural
- Written as a consulting manager coordinating work across teams

Do not:
- Over-explain
- Use casual slang
- Sound uncertain or overly deferential
